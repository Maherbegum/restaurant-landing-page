Banana Pancakes

Whisk egg in a bowl and add butter, honey and vanilla essence to it, Whisk well. Add mashed banana to it then mix it well. Now shift flour and baking powder to the mixture and then mix them well to not let any lumps form. Keep adding milk to the mixture while whisking until a smooth paste. Now heat a pan and pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake. Cook until pancakes are golden brown on both sides.Finally, Serve pancakes hot with sliced banana slices on top.

Prep time

30 min

Cook time

15 min

Servings

6 servings

Step 1

Step 3

After that heat the pan, pour mixture in a circular way then let it cook perfectly after that relish them!
